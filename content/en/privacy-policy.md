### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
**We** use Cookies and other similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and stash away certain info. The tracking technologies we deploy include beacons, tags, and scripts, all used to gather and track info, and to fine-tune and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file dropped onto your Device. You can tell your browser to give all Cookies the boot or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But hey, if you don't accept Cookies, you might miss out on some cool parts of our Service. Unless you've set your browser to refuse Cookies, our Service will likely use them.
- **Web Beacons**: Certain parts of our Service and our emails might contain these little electronic files called web beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). They let the Company, for example, count users who've checked out those pages or opened an email, and they're also awesome for other related website stats (like tracking how popular a certain section is and making sure systems and servers are all good).

**Cookies** can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even after you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.
**We** use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are an absolute must for hooking you up with services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They're key for authenticating users and keeping a lid on fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for simply wouldn't happen, and we only use 'em to deliver those specific services to you.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are all about figuring out if users have given the green light to the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're rocking the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you hit up the Website.

**For more info** about the Cookies we use and your options regarding Cookies, just swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
